TI  - Exploiting structure similarity in refinement: automated NCS and target-structure restraints in BUSTER
AB  - Local structural similarity restraints (LSSR) provide a novel method for
      exploiting NCS or structural similarity to an external target structure. Two
      examples are given where BUSTER re-refinement of PDB entries with LSSR produces
      marked improvements, enabling further structural features to be modelled.
